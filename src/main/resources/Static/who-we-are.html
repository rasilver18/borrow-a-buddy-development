Who We Are

Let your stress melt away...Borrow a buddy from us today!

Why Borrow-A-Buddy?

Are you having a stressful week at work?

Feeling lonely after a breakup?

Or just down in the blues about socially-distant life?

Borrow a buddy to play with for a few hours, and feel the stress melt away!

Research has shown that simply petting a dog or cat lowers levels of cortisol - the stress hormone - and can even increase levels of oxytocin - the feel-good hormone.

Borrow-A-Buddy is the perfect solution for seniors stuck at home, young adults who live alone, or even families who just need something - anything - to add some excitement to their day.

While some hospitals and schools use pet therapy to help their patients, Borrow-A-Buddy makes the enjoyment of a pet accessible to everyone.

Making the enjoyment of a pet accessible to everyone

We launched Borrow-A-Buddy in 2020, the year when everyone was stuck at home!

Borrow-A-Buddy exists to connect pet lovers in need of companionship with pet owners who are willing to share their furry best friends.

Answer a few short questions about your personality and preferences, and you'll be matched with a compatible animal in your area!
