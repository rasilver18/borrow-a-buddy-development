Our Safety Standards

At Borrow-A-Buddy we take the safety of our clients and pets very seriously. By clicking the boxes below you can learn more about the steps we take to protect our pets, owners, and community

In order to protect the Borrow-A-Buddy community we have set the following safety practices:

Both the borrower and the pet owner must receive third party referrals. These referrals are reviewed by Borrow-A-Buddy staff before you can be approved to use our site.

We know that your booking is important to you. If a pet owner needs to cancel your pet booking, we will ensure a replacement booking within 12 hours. Owners and borrows that do not show up for a booking get a strike on their record.

We are here to make sure your experience is positive. We provide consistent support through our "Contact Us" page as well as our emergency line. Please contact us if your experience was anything but positive.

We know that security is important. Not only do we ensure that your information is secure on our site from unwanted parties, but we also make sure that you are secure. Please review our code of conduct for more ways that we ensure this.

Borrow-A-Buddy is a caring, safe community. To ensure it stays that way we have the following expectations:

  1. Be respectful when communicating with users.
  2. Be available for emergencies through our hotline.
  3. Promote the integrity and safety of the platform by reviewing all applications.
  4. Display reviews of pet owners and borrowers.
  5. Take appropriate action for all concerns that are communicated.
  6. Provide information protection for users.
  7. Secure a new booking for users if a booking falls through.

We thank you for being a part of Borrow-a-Buddy and continuing to make it the incredible community it has become!
