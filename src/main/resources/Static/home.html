WELCOME TO BORROW-A-BUDDY

The first community that allows everyone to enjoy the companionship of a pet

HOW THIS WORKS

Answer a few questions about yourself and the type of pet you're looking to borrow. We'll recommend pets that meet your needs and preferences and then allow you to learn more about each one. We are confident that you will find the perfect buddy!

EXPECTATIONS

While we pride ourselves on being an inclusive community, we also believe in keeping both our pets and their human counterparts safe. As such we expect that all pets and humans adhere to our strict Code of Conduct policy.
